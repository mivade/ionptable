Ion Periodic Table
==================

These pages contain information on candidate ion species for use in
ion trap quantum computing and other atomic physics experiments. It is
originally based on pages maintained and hosted by the `Monroe
group`_. You can download all the source files on GitHub_.

.. _Monroe group: http://iontrap.umd.edu/
.. _GitHub: https://github.com/mivade/ionptable
